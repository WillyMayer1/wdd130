A little about me

Next, he moved with his family to Venezuela but when he started to make his family (when he married my grandmother and have their children) he showed his family about the mate, and I was born knowing this custom.

Now, I started to live here in Argentina (main mate drinkers) and I learned more about this custom (about how to drink it, what you can add it, and so on) and now. I want that all the people can learn about this
