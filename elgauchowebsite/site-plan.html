Overview

Purpose

El gaucho website is created to get to know more about Argentine culture, especially talking about "mates", a traditional drink, which is very drunk in Argentina. To drink mate it is necessary to know some basics because it is part of a custom in this country, and helps to strengthen family relationships and friendships in a healthy, no addictive, and inclusive way. Everyone using our website can learn more about what a mate is composed of, how can drink it, its history, and what is some of its nutritional benefits. The way to drink mate was evolving throughout the years, being capable to add or change some ways to drink it, for example, exist the cold mate called "tereré" or adding fruit peel to change the flavor. If you learn all these things, you'll be prepared to be a "gaucho" (argentine country person).

Audience

El gaucho website is focused on everyone who wants to know more about Argentine culture, especially a traditional South American drink called "mate" which all the people in Argentina drink. Everyone can access our website through smartphones and computers with different measures (responsive) and can learn and enjoy it no matter age, gender, etc.

Content

Home page

Introduction

The primary purpose of my website is to announce to visitors, or viewers, some of the Argentinian cultures, especially about the “mate”, which is a traditional drink in this part of the continent. With this website, everyone who wants to visit Argentina in the future will have an idea about what to expect if they want to visit it one day, or if they want to know some of the things that Argentinian people do.

How to prepare mate

One of the activities that I want that viewers do on my website is to make a specific tour, showing first some pictures of the typical vessel (called “mate”) sometimes made with wood, clay, or any other material that could resist hot drinks. And then, we will show the special ingredient called “yerba mate” (or “yerba” to shortcut) which is a specific cut and prepared herb. After showing the mate and yerba mate, the website shows how to prepare the mate , starting by filling the mate with the yerba, next, we will introduce and specific straw called “bombilla”, which is only to drink mate, the instructions will say that we need to put the straw into the mate, and then, we can fulfill the mate with hot water. This is the main way to prepare mate, if the customer prefers, he or she can add sugar, spices, and fruit peel to change the flavor.

About Mate

Answering questions

Some of the questions that viewers could make are, principal, “what is mate? How could I drink this? Is this healthy?” Other questions that viewers could make is “What is the history of this kind of drink? Is this a culture, and, there is a way about how to drink it?” Our website will be prepared to answer these questions and more.

Some history & information

And now, how our visitors or viewers know how to prepare mate, we will show some of the history (just principal) about this drink. We will show why is so important for Argentinian people to drink this with family instead alone. And, if the visitor wants, why he or she could consider adopting this tradition. Also, it will contain a section where it will explain in detail all the nutritional functions it contains so that anyone (especially people with knowledge in nutrition) can know what properties it contains.

A little about me and contact me

And, at last, a section that everyone can contact me through my social media and email to do specific questions or if he or she wants more information about it. I will explain a little about my history knowing this culture, learned by the country people of this part of Argentina. Because I (and possible everyone who did not visit Argentina before) did not learn some of these basics before, and, when I participated in a meeting with Argentinian friends, I drank it in a way that some of the guests showed a little impressed because the way that I was drinking mate it is not the right way, and then show me how to drink it correctly.

Maybe you will think “why is this website important?”. One of the things that will distinguish our website from others is that we will prepare a complete, but simple, tour about the mate. We will show not only how to prepare it, but how this culture started and how this changed throughout the years, our webpage will answer some of the typical questions in a simple way that everyone can understand and share this info with others. This drink is very common in some countries in South America (for example Chile, Uruguay, Paraguay, and Argentina) with this website, you can use this information if you consider visiting some of the countries before too.
